About Us.

Flowers that make you happy for a long time

Are you looking for a great solution for the interior of your flat, house, office or shop? We have it! We are AuraDecor in Bratislava and we specialize in decorating events and rooms for any goals. We make large flowers from a material that is comfortable to look after (izolon, foam). We are ready to embody any of your fantasies. As we are at the beginning of our journey, we are offering nice prices for you. We also offer the rental of our products. We are open to cooperation and are ready to keep creating together with you.
